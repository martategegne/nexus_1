My Work Journey

Academic & Learning Timeline

Early Education

Grades 1 – 8

I began my educational journey at Aziba Primary School, where I developed a strong foundation in discipline, curiosity, and responsibility toward learning.

Secondary Education

Grades 9 – 12 (2020 – 2023)

I continued my studies at Dessie Special Boarding School (DSBS). This period strengthened my academic confidence, independence, and ability to work under pressure.

2024 – Present

In 2024, I joined Addis Ababa University as an undergraduate student. This stage marks the beginning of my professional preparation in technology, problem-solving, and software development.

Skills Development

Along my journey, I have continuously worked on improving both my technical and personal skills.

  • Team collaboration and communication
  • Self-learning and adaptability

Challenges & Lessons Learned

My journey has not been without challenges. Adapting to new environments, managing academic pressure, and learning complex concepts taught me resilience and patience.

“Every challenge I faced became a lesson that strengthened my confidence and shaped my growth.”

Looking Ahead

I aspire to continue building my skills, gaining practical experience, and contributing positively through technology. My goal is to graduate prepared, confident, and ready to serve society through innovation.
